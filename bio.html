Brief Bio-Data of Dr. T Sudheer Kumar

  • Dr. T Sudheer Kumar obtained his B.E in Electronics and Communication Engineering, M.Tech in Radar and Microwave Engineering from Andhra University College of Engineering, Andhra University, Visakhapatnam.
  • He obtained his Ph.D in the field of Antenna Array Optimization and Pattern Synthesis from Andhra University in 2015.
  • He has 20 years of Teaching and Research Experience.
  • At present, he is working as Associate Professor in ECE, Shri Vishnu Engineering College for Women (Autonomous).
  • He has published 67 Research Articles in various peer-reviewed International Journals and Conferences.
  • He was the Member of Academic Senate, Andhra University during 2013-2015.
  • Dr. Sudheer is a Senior Member of IEEE, Fellow of IETE, Life Member of Institution of Engineering (India), Society of EMI/EMC (India), ISTE.
  • He is Chairman, Education Society Chapter, IEEE Vizag Section from 2020 to Till date.
  • He was Executive Committee Member, Education Society Chapter, IEEE Hyderabad Section 2020.
  • He is Executive Committee Member, Institution of Engineers (India), Bhimavaram Local Center for the period 2019 – Till Date.
  • He is Executive Committee Member, IETE Kakinada Center for the period 2022 – 2024.
  • He is a Reviewer of Microwave and Optical Technology Letters, Wiley ETRI Journal, and Journal of Applied Computational Electromagnetic Society (ACES), Springer Journal of Soft Computing, several IEEE Conferences.
  • He Chaired Special Sessions in Springer International Conference on Micro-Electronics, Electromagnetics and Telecommunications ICMEET – 2016, 2017, 2019 & 2021.
  • He is Chairman, Technical Programme Committee, Springer ICMEET – 2022 Conference organized by Department of ECE, SVECW(A) during 23 – 24 July 2022.
  • He is a Reviewer of Optical Communication books by Greg Kiezer and P Chakravarthy, published by Tata McGraw-Hill Education.
  • He has Delivered Several Invited Talks on Research and Innovation, Machine Learning, Nature Inspired Optimization Techniques for Engineering Problems, Outcome Based Education Framework, Antenna Array Optimization, OFDM Basics for Wireless Communications, Research Paper Writing and Manuscript Preparation.
  • He received Best Teacher Award for the Year 2018 for his outstanding contribution towards Best practices in Teaching.
  • His area of Interest includes Machine Learning, Wireless Communication, Antenna Array Optimization, Outcome Based Education, and Innovative Teaching Methodologies.
  • He Guided Three Ph.Ds under his Supervision and Currently guiding One Research Scholar.
  • At present he holds Administrative responsibilities at SVECW like:
    • Chairman, Academic Audit
    • Coordinator: NBA and NAAC
    • IEEE Student Branch Counselor
    • Tech Guru Coordinator – SVECW AICTE Idea Lab
